When this policy is active, iframe content will not be able use tools like touch-action: none and e.preventDefault() on touchstartevents to prevent the content frome scrolling. When it's off, iframes can use those tools (as normal) to prevent scrolling.

The frame uses body{touch-action:none} to prevent its content from scrolling. However, when the policy is "on", the iframe content will still be scrollable! The policy prevents touch-action:none from working.
